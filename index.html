Wesley's Portfolio AKA Dice Jones

My name is Wesley Jones, everyone calls me Dice. I'm a double amputee quadriplegic. I've been paralyzed since 2015 after being gunned down three times at a peace gathering down in Florida. In 2016 I bought a laptop and the equipment that I could use to be able to use a computer since then I have came a far away of understanding the ends and outs of a computer. Now I'm pursuing a career in web developing. My passions are traveling , music, skydiving and making dreams come true. I am self motivated. I don't let my disability get the better of me. I started jumping out of planes a couple yearss ago as a new hobbie I fell in love with it. It's my addiction. so far I have jumped out of 19 planes and counting
