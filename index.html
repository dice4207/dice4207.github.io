My name is Wesley Jones, everyone calls me Dice. I'm a double amputee quadriplegic. I've been paralyzed since 2015 after being gunned down three times at a peace gathering down in Florida. In 2016 I bought a laptop and the equipment to be able to use a computer since then I have came a long aways of understanding the ins and outs of a computer. Now I'm pursuing a career in web developing. My passions are traveling , music, skydiving and making dreams come true. I am self motivated. My gaol is to become the best man that I can be. So one day I can share my knowledge. To perhaps maybe change someones life for the better. I don't let my disability get the better of me. I started jumping out of planes a couple years ago .
