Wesley's Portfolio AKA Dice Jones

My name is Wesley Jones, everyone calls me Dice. I'm a double amputee quadriplegic. I've been paralyzed since 2015 after being gunned down three times at a peace gathering down in Florida. In 2016 I bought a laptop and the equipment that I could use to be able to use a computer since then I have came a far away of understanding the ins and outs of a computer. Now I'm pursuing a career in web developing. My passions are traveling , music, skydiving and making dreams come true. I am self motivated. I don't let my disability get the better of me. I started jumping out of planes a couple years ago. The first time I fell in love with it. I have jump out of 19 planes so far. When I'm passionate about something it means everything to me. With that being said I know I will climb the ladders in web developing to the top. The sky is the limit.
